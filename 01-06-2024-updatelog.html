version 2.00.055

We are happy to release the version 2.00.055 There are many
changes in this version.So we list all changes below as many
as we can. Please take your time to read it.
[New]
United States:
Cruiser Brooklyn ACR
Battleship Florida
Battleship Wyoming
Battleship Oklahoma 1927
Attack aircraft carrier Langley
Fleet aircraft carrier Lexington
Submarine S-class (US) now open
Japan:
Attack aircraft carrier: Hōshō
Fleet carrier: Akagi (formerly known as Akagi in the game and renamed as Akagi 1942)
Submarine Ro-33 Submarine
Submarine Type I-1 Submarine Now Open

[Activity]
To celebrate the new year, we have launched a special double reward activity! As long as you participate in the specified mode of battle at the specified time, you can receive additional rewards such as silver coins, medals, and experience after the battle ends
(Please check the details of the activity interface)

[Balance]
The number of some torpedoes has been adjusted
Cancel the components of the air defense computer and replace the original components with "side armor belts" (suffer torpedo damage -5%)
[Game mechanics]
The game scale has been changed from 1:2 to 1:1
The trajectory formula has been modified, making the current trajectory smoother, but the hit determination will not undergo significant changes as a result
Each ship can now automatically calculate the air defense distance
"Over range detection" can no longer detect attacking aircraft carriers and fleet aircraft carriers
